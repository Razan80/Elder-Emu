Funding & Packages

Most of your care is already paid for

The Australian Government funds a significant portion of home care costs through the Support at Home program. Our team will explain exactly what you're entitled to — in plain English, in your language.

Check my funding Call 1300 226 574
Funding options

Three ways to pay for care

Whether you're government-funded or paying privately, we make it work for you.

Recommended

Support at Home

The main government program with 8 funding levels from $11,000 to $78,000 per year. Clinical care is 100% funded. Personal care becomes fully funded from October 2026. You receive a quarterly budget to spend on services.

Commonwealth Home Support Programme

Entry-level government-funded support for people who need basic help at home. A great starting point while you wait for a full Support at Home package.

Fastest Start

Private Pay — Start This Week

No waitlist, no assessment, no government process. Pay directly and start receiving care within 48 hours. Ideal while waiting for government funding to be approved.

Getting started

How to access your funding

Four simple steps — and we'll walk you through every one.

1

Call My Aged Care

Call 1800 200 422 or let Elder Emu do it for you — we'll start the process on your behalf.

2

Free Assessment

Complete a free assessment to determine your support level. We can be there with you.

3

Receive Your Funding

Receive your funding approval and quarterly budget from the government.

4

Choose Elder Emu

Choose Elder Emu as your registered provider and begin care immediately.

Big news for October 2026

Personal care services (showering, dressing, grooming) will become 100% government-funded — meaning zero cost for eligible Support at Home clients. Ask us how to get approved before October so you're ready when the change takes effect.
