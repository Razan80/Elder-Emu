Personal care services

Every aspect of your daily routine, handled with dignity and care.

Showering & Bathing

Safe, assisted bathing with full respect for your modesty and comfort.

Grooming & Hair Care

Help with hair washing, styling, shaving and personal presentation.

Dressing & Undressing

Gentle support choosing clothes and getting dressed for the day.

Toileting & Continence

Discreet, respectful assistance with toileting and continence needs.

Oral Hygiene

Help with teeth cleaning, denture care and oral health routines.

Skin Care & Pressure Care

Moisturising, skin checks and pressure area care to protect your wellbeing.

Big news for October 2026

From 1 October 2026, personal care services become fully funded by the Australian Government under Support at Home — meaning $0 out-of-pocket cost for eligible clients. Ask us how to get approved before October so you're ready when it begins.

Your comfort matters

A familiar face every visit

We know that having a different carer each time can feel uncomfortable — especially for personal care. That's why we match you with the same carers and keep them on your roster for the long term.

  • Same carers assigned to you every visit
  • Flexible scheduling around your routine
  • Male or female carers available on request
  • Culturally and linguistically diverse team
  • Supervised by Registered Nurses
