Our difference

Why choose Elder Emu nursing care?

  • All nurses hold current AHPRA registration
  • Founding team with hospital & aged care experience
  • Connected to Liverpool Hospital discharge pathways
  • 100% government funded under Support at Home
  • Consistent same-nurse visits for familiarity
  • Available for complex and palliative care needs

$0 out-of-pocket for nursing care

Clinical nursing is 100% funded by the Australian Government under Support at Home. You pay $0 for nursing visits — no matter your income or assets. Our team will help you access this funding quickly and without stress.
