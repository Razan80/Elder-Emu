About Elder Emu Home Care

Built by nurses. Driven by love.

Elder Emu Home Care was founded by a family with deep roots in aged care and nursing. We started this business because we saw what good care looks like — and what it looks like when it falls short. We chose to do something about it.

Our story

A family built on care

It started around a dinner table in Western Sydney. Three Registered Nurses — a wife, a sister with aged care management experience, and a sister-in-law with hospital and community care experience — talking about the gap they kept seeing: elderly Australians getting care that was adequate on paper, but fell short in practice. Care that ticked boxes but didn't feel warm. Carers who changed every week. Providers who were hard to reach when families had questions.

They decided to build something different. Elder Emu Home Care was founded on the belief that home care should be genuinely led by nurses — not just supervised by them from a distance. Every care plan is designed by a Registered Nurse. Every carer is matched personally. Every family gets a real phone number that someone actually answers.

Based in Parramatta, we serve families right across Greater Western Sydney — from Blacktown to Liverpool, Penrith to the Hills. We're not a national franchise or a call centre. We're your neighbours, and we take that responsibility seriously. Because when it's your mum or your dad or your grandparent, the standard of care shouldn't be "good enough." It should be excellent.
