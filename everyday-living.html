Understanding costs

Means-tested co-contributions for everyday living

Domestic assistance and everyday living services are subject to a means-tested co-contribution under the Support at Home program. This means your share depends on your income and assets — and for many clients, the cost is very low or $0.

Our team will walk you through exactly what your co-contribution will be, in plain English, before care begins. No surprises, no confusion.
